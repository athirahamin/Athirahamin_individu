COMMON INDIAN MAIN DISH

BANANA LEAF RICE

Banana leaf rice is a traditional method of serving rice dishes in South Indian cuisine. Due to the migration of South Indians, banana leaf rice can also be found in areas with significant ethnic South Indian diaspora such as Malaysia and Singapore

CHICKEN TIKKA MASALA

Chicken tikka masala is a dish of chunks of roasted marinated chicken in a spiced curry. The curry is usually creamy and orange-coloured

BUTTER CHICKEN

Butter chicken or murg makhani is a dish of chicken in a spiced tomato, butter and cream sauce. It originated in India
