COMMON MALAY MAIN DISH

RENDANG

Rendang is a rich and tender coconut beef stew which is explosively flavorful and beef rendang is the most popular rendang

NASI LEMAK

Malaysian coconut milk rice, served with sambal, fried crispy anchovies, toasted peanuts and cucumber. This is the best and most authentic nasi lemak recipe

NASI KERABU

A popular dish during the month of Ramadhan, Nasi Kerabu Kelantan, accompanied with a variety of fresh herbs and raw vegetables is high in fibre which promotes good digestive health – something that’s encouraged during the fasting month
